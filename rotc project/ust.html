Marshal Ustinov

Russian Navy - Marshal Ustinov is a Slava-class cruiser designed during the Soviet Union as a Raketnyy Kreyser or "anti- ship rocket cruiser. As originally constructed the vessel had a standard displacement of 10,000 tons and 12,700 tons at full load. By 2009, this had decreased to 9,530 tons and 11,670 tons at full load. The vessel is powered by a combined gas or gas system comprising four 23,300 kilowatts boost gas turbines and two 8,900 kW cruise gas turbines driving two shafts for a combined 93,000 kW. This gives the cruiser a maximum speed of 34 knots.
